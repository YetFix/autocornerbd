About Us

Background

Auto Corner was established by Eng. Md Shohidul Islam (Father of present Managing Director) in 1973 along with its showroom in the center of Dhaka. Their business was growing up sharply with demand of Automobile sector of Bangladesh. For the time being, more products had been added with the tire business. Up to 1998, Auto Corner was a business entity for trading of different Automobile and spare parts items to individual customers. From the beginning of 2000 millennium, we enlisted in different defense institute in Bangladesh and started supplying of tire and spare parts.

MISSION

To supply world class products and services according to the asking requirement of defense Authority through the evolution process. We aim to solidify Auto Corner’s position as the country’s best defense supplier for defense services and equipment.

Vission

To support the Bangladesh’s defense and security forces through introducing renown Company with advanced technologies and professional personnel in this era. Our slogan is – Supplying Solution Support.
